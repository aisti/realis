# Realis Team 2 notes
## Session 1: ETYM
2026/07/04

We board the mail carrier's Orphan Vessel "Queen of Peace" and set out for Etym, on the outskirts of Suzerainty territory.  The journey will take 3 days.  We get to know one another as Caprice gets their hands in every metaphorical cookie jar aboard.

As we reach the moon, the Farmers Guild hails and promptly breaches our Orphan Vessel in order to sap energy.  A massive, diamond-hard acorn lodges in the hull and periodically regrows roots and vines into the bulkheads, seeking always the power core. Caprice prunes it back from taking over the ship, and Coyote tries to send the light of the Drift back to the First Root, the great tree that grows from Realis.  Contact overwhelms it, sapping its energy and satisfying the pirates.

Etym: Liquid metal, organic shapes, flowing.  Pony finds us a secluded cavern to use as base of operations after checking in with the local post office.  The whole moon is blockaded by Farmer biotech.  Last's gonne draws her to their base and we scare off their guards before breaking in.  

We work together to reverse the jammer and use it to report in to Lord Crownguard, informing him of the problem with the Farmers Guild and requesting backup.  The Suzerainty comes in to establish order.

## Session 2: ~~THE STRING OF PEARLS~~ HEIST ON ORIONELLE
2026/07/11

We spend downtime on Etym trying to repair the damage to the Queen of Peace.

Tasked with securing an alliance with House Fyonn of the String of Pearls before the Sphenic Polytechnic can beat us to it, we call on an old flame(?) of Caprice's, Tessa Fyonn, and entice her with a night of excitement pulling a heist at a fashion auction.

The plan is going off without a hitch and everyone is safely infiltrated thanks to Colton. Coyote convinces a model to create space for Tessa in the show when Last's instincts take over and kill her in cold blood in front of everyone.  Panicking under spectral searchlights, Coyote pulls them both into the Drift where they are set upon by creature of the Drift ATROS, UNSPOKEN CRUELTY, who drives it to make Last question whether they truly exist as a person. 

Tessa snags the model's dress as it flutters to the floor.  She is decidedly not into Caprice, her heart set on a commoner whom she has decided to neg at a distance.  

We have not succeeded.  However, we HAVE secured... well, maybe a favor.  Blackmail, perhaps?  An ally for future escapades?  We haven't *truly* surprised Daddy Crownguard one way or the other.
